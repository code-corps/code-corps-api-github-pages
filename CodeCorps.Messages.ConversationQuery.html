    active_filter(Ecto.Queryable.t(), map()) :: Ecto.Queryable.t()

    Filters CodeCorps.Conversation record queries to return only those considered to be active.

    Active conversations belong either:

    • to a CodeCorps.Message initiated by user
    • to a CodeCorps.Message initiated by an admin, with at least a single conversation part
